## Market Analysis

### Cameroon Construction Industry (2025-2026)
- **Market Size:** ~$1.1-1.3 billion annually (single-family housing sector)
- **Growth Rate:** 3.5-6.3% annually projected through 2029
- **GDP Contribution:** 6.1% of national GDP
- **Employment:** ~675,000+ workers in construction sector
- **Key Driver:** Government infrastructure investment (CFA1.9 trillion in 2025 Public Investment Budget)

### Industry Segments in Cameroon
1. **Residential Construction** - Housing (bungalows, duplexes, apartments)
2. **Commercial Buildings** - Offices, warehouses, shops
3. **Public Works** - Roads, bridges, drainage systems
4. **Civil Engineering** - Schools, hospitals, government buildings
5. **Renovation & Finishing** - Painting, tiling, interior work

### Target Regions
- **Primary:** Douala (economic capital, Littoral region)
- **Secondary:** Yaoundé (political capital, Centre region)
- **Coverage:** All 10 regions of Cameroon
